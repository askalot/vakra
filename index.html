About us

vakra is a contemporary Sydney based band comprised of local artists.

The word vakra literally stands for what we want to be—deviant. We aren't defined by language, geography or genres of music.

A cocktail of like minded individuals who are passionate about music; the vakra team comes from different parts of the world and walks of life. Enjoy our unique blend!
